Our Story

"We love because He first loved us." - John 4:19

We are both priveleged to have encoutered the love of Jesus in our lives and we believe that it is through His love that we have found love in each other.

We first met at a Catholic Community which we both formed part of. Since our early days of our relationship, we always put God in the center of our relationship. We believe that the gift of spiritual intimacy has continued to strengthen our bond because we allowed God to be the hardening agent.

Just as we have experienced God's love, we would like our marriage to be an opportunity to share God's love with our guests. We pray that the love of God will fill your hearts.

May God bless you abundantly!

Bernice & Joel
